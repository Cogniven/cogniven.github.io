Archive Timeline Pass

Feb 18, 2026

A partial timeline layout draft for sequencing archive records by decision moments.

This draft explored a timeline-first archive view. Instead of grouping by category, entries were arranged by transition events such as deprecation, split, and migration.

The model improved historical readability but remained incomplete because metadata quality was inconsistent across existing items. Several records lacked clear temporal anchors.

The page concept was paused and replaced by category lists, while the timeline idea remains reserved for a future archive mode.
