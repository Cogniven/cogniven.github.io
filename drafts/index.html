Personal Knowledge Atlas

A structured map of knowledge fields, entries, concepts, references, and depth marks.

Overview

This page records fields of knowledge I have encountered and organized. It does not grade broad domains as a whole; instead, each specific entry carries its own depth mark.

Standard

Knowledge Display Rules

Domains classify knowledge. Entries carry depth.

System Intent

The atlas is designed to show what kinds of knowledge have been encountered, how they are organized, and where they connect with writing, research, creation, and judgment.

Core Structure

Domain

A broad container for grouping related knowledge entries.

Entry

A specific field, concept group, tradition, or body of knowledge.

Anchors

Concepts, authors, works, theories, cases, or methods that define the entry.

Optional Notes

Notes, sources, and linked projects are added only when they clarify the entry.

Depth Scale

Entry Levels

The color of the level badge becomes stronger as the entry becomes more usable.

Knowledge Domains

Ten broad domains are used as a stable frame for organizing specific knowledge entries.
