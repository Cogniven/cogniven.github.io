About

This site is a long-term personal structure that gathers work, ideas, systems, and research directions that continue to evolve. The material exists in different states: some pieces are relatively complete, while others are still being revised, reorganized, or expanded. It functions more like an ongoing workspace than a fixed presentation. What matters here is not only the outcome, but also how things are structured, clarified through revision, and allowed to develop over time.

Profile

My work moves across several intersecting directions, including research, programming, design, writing, and experimental creation. These areas are not treated as separate domains, but as different ways of engaging with similar questions. Some work takes the form of text, some becomes systems or tools, and some appears as visual or auditory expression. Beyond the medium itself, I am primarily concerned with how things are constructed and how clarity can be maintained within complexity.

Focus

The work here tends to revolve around several recurring directions. One is analytical, involving reading, comparison, and structuring complex material. Another is technical, focusing on tools, systems, and long-term frameworks. A third is more expressive, working through image, sound, and form. These directions are not isolated; they intersect and shift across different projects, forming new combinations over time.

Approach

I usually begin with structure and then move into the content itself. Categories, naming, and organization are continuously adjusted along the way. Revision is an ongoing process: some parts are rewritten, some are reorganized, and others are removed. The site itself follows the same logic—it operates as a continuously edited environment rather than a finished endpoint.
