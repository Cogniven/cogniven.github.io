Arkham Narrative Conversion

A project for turning play sessions of Arkham Horror into narrative material through recording, transcription, restructuring, and later AI-assisted rewriting. Recent updates include early image experiments based on selected moments from The Dunwich Legacy.

Visual test added Updated: 2026-05-21 Single Project
Premise Workflow Current Stage Image Tests Extensions

Section 01

Premise

This project begins from actual play. The goal is not only to record what happened in the game, but to turn the session into narrative material that can later be rewritten, expanded, and selectively visualized.

Play Session as Source Material

active

The primary material comes from actual play sessions of Arkham Horror, treated as the first layer of narrative input.

Scenario and Rule Context

draft

Section 02

Workflow

The workflow is planned as a staged conversion process: gameplay, recording, transcription, narrative structuring, selective image testing, and later literary rewriting.

Audio Recording Plan

draft

planned

Future sessions will be recorded in audio form so that spoken reactions, decisions, and emergent dramatic moments can be preserved.

Transcription and Story Sorting

draft

planned

Recordings will be converted into text, then reorganized together with scenario material into a readable story sequence.

AI-Assisted Novelization

test

later stage

After the narrative material is stabilized, AI will be used to help rewrite it into a more novel-like form while keeping the original session logic intact.

Section 04

Image Tests

These images are early attempts to visualize selected fragments from the first Arkham Horror campaign cycle, The Dunwich Legacy. They are currently used as supplementary material for the project, not as a finished visual series.

At the Clover Club

An attempt to cheat at the Clover Club, preserved as an early visual fragment from the campaign.

Miskatonic Dormitory

A scene in which the investigators fight off a monster inside the student dormitory of Miskatonic University.

La Bella Luna

A moment of reckless drinking at La Bella Luna, kept as a lighter but still unstable fragment of the adventure.

Museum Security Office

The investigators attempt to wake the guard outside the museum security office before moving deeper into the case.

Night Entry

A night-time infiltration of the museum, marking the shift from investigation into direct risk.

Restricted Hall

The Necronomicon is found inside the museum's restricted hall, turning the investigation toward forbidden knowledge.

Train to Dunwich

The investigators board the train to Dunwich, carrying the unresolved danger of Arkham into the countryside.

Incident on the Train

A dangerous moment erupts while the train is still running, breaking the journey into sudden panic and action.

Arrival at Arkham

The investigators arrive at Arkham, where the route of the campaign begins to connect scattered clues into a larger threat.

Bloodstained Altar

A bloodstained altar is found in Arkham, giving physical shape to the ritual danger behind the campaign.

Against the Unspeakable

The investigators attempt to use magic against an unspeakable monster, closing the current visual sequence with confrontation and uncertainty.
