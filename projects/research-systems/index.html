Research Systems

A structured environment for long-term inquiry. This section organizes research as systems of themes, modules, methods, and evolving lines of work.

Overview

Unlike a narrative project page, a research system describes a framework: what is being studied, how it is divided, and how its parts connect over time.

System Intent

The research system provides a stable structure for long-term inquiry, allowing topics, methods, and materials to evolve without losing coherence.
