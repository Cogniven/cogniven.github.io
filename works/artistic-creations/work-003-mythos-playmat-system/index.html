Mythos Playmat System

A custom playmat design for Arkham Horror: The Card Game, combining functional card zones, occult ornamentation, and a printable tabletop layout.

Type

Playmat / tabletop interface design

Format

7500 × 4500 px · suggested print 150 × 90 cm

Card size

Designed around 90 mm × 65 mm sleeved cards

Overview

This work is a fan-made playmat layout for Arkham Horror: The Card Game. It treats the play surface as a tabletop interface: player areas, scenario areas, encounter zones, discard zones, and common supply positions are arranged into a single symmetrical ritual map.

Concept

A functional ritual map

The design combines two layers: a practical layer for card placement and a visual layer based on cosmic horror, occult diagrams, and weathered ornamental framing. The goal is to make the table easier to manage while preserving the atmosphere of an investigation board.

The layout is built for a two-sided table arrangement. Player areas are mirrored across the upper and lower edges, while shared scenario and encounter zones are placed on the side so that the play state remains readable during a campaign session.

Slots

Functional card zones

The slot text follows the Chinese labels used in the current print draft.

Player areas

The top and bottom edges are arranged as mirrored player zones. Each side contains slots for investigator-related play: player card area, hand reference positions, ally, body, accessory, spell slots, asset/event/skill areas, draw pile, discard pile, blood/SAN tracking reference, and resource/clue reference.

  • 角色卡 / Investigator card
  • 手部 1、手部 2 / Hand slots 1–2
  • 盟友、身体、饰品 / Ally, body, accessory
  • 法术 1、法术 2 / Spell slots 1–2
  • 事件牌、技能牌、资产牌 / Event, skill, asset areas
  • 摸牌堆、弃牌堆 / Draw pile and discard pile
  • 血 / SAN、资源 / 线索 / Health, sanity, resources, clues

Scenario and encounter areas

The left side is reserved for shared game-state components, keeping scenario progression and encounter handling separate from the player card zones.

  • 密谋卡牌 / Agenda cards
  • 密谋牌 / Agenda deck
  • 场景牌 / Act cards
  • 剧本牌 / Scenario reference card
  • 遭遇牌堆 / Encounter deck
  • 弃牌堆 / Encounter discard pile
  • 胜利区 / Victory display

Symmetry and table flow

The composition is horizontally symmetrical, allowing two investigators or two play sides to face each other. The central circle is left open as the main play space, suitable for locations, enemies, clue tokens, and temporary scenario components.

Print margin

A black border is intentionally left around the artwork. This gives the print shop additional tolerance for cropping, edge binding, or minor cutting deviation, while keeping the visible play area away from the trim edge.

Print Specification

Size and scaling

Scale

50 px / cm · approx. 127 DPI

Card basis

90 mm × 65 mm cards

The current layout is calculated around 90 mm × 65 mm cards. If the printed size changes, the card slots scale proportionally. For example, printing at 120 cm wide gives a scale ratio of 120 / 150 = 0.800, so a 90 mm card slot becomes about 72.0 mm. Printing at 150 cm × 88 cm keeps the intended physical card fit.
