Selection Logic

What belongs here.

Not every output needs to be exhibited. The aim is not volume, but definition. Works included here should have a visible form, a completed state, or a recognizable direction.

Over time, each entry may grow into a standalone page with images, notes, context, and revisions. For now, this page serves as the opening index.
